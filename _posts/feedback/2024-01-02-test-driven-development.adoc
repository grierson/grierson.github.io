== TL;DR

- Apply the scientific method in small steps, writing down your experiments
- Breakdown and simplify the problem.
- (Rich Hickey) - Father Wattson's questions
  - Where are you at?
  - Where are you going?
  - What do you know?
  - What do you need to know?
